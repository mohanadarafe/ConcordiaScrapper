General Dentistry

Dr. Wisdom VSL offers all dental services to adults and children. We believe in prevention so we offer cleaning, polishing, fillings, and all spheres of dental medicine therapy.

Learn More

Invisalign

Invisalign is an aesthetic alternative to conventional braces. Invisalign aligns teeth through the use of a series of transparent and personalized aligners. Invisalign is an invisible, discreet and removable solution.

Learn More

Implants
The dental implant is an artificial titanium root attached to the bone to replace a missing tooth. This root will support a crown. Success is based on an advanced case study and a rigorous surgical method.

Learn More

Treatment of Canals
The basic treatment if the nerve of a tooth is infected or if it suffers from irreversible inflammation. It can be prescribed if a tooth is fractured due to trauma and allows a natural tooth to be preserved.

Learn More

Wisdom Teeth
In the back of the mouth, the wisdom teeth served our ancestors. When these teeth lack space to push properly, they cause several problems requiring their extraction.

Learn More

Conscious Sedation
Even today, visiting the dentist and anxiety go hand in hand. If you or your children fall into this category, conscious nitrous oxide sedation is an excellent remedy for this problem, avoiding general anesthesia.

Learn More

Aesthetic Dental Care
Beauty is a desirable thing, and few people escape this desire. Whether bleaching, Invisalign, porcelain veneers, or rehabilitating worn teeth, we are listening.

Learn More

Crowns & Bridges
A crown or bridge is a fixed denture that restores the shape, appearance and function of a tooth. A bridge can replace one or more missing teeth and restore a lost function.

Learn More
